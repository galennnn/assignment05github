Project

Self-Chopping Tree

I biomedically engineered a tree that chops itself right before dying. It prevents deforestation and makes lumberjack-work nonexistent. I do hope it doesn't render my lumberjacking degree worthless when I get it.

Double-Bladed Chainsaw

The double-bladed chainsaw allows for the chopping of two trees at once. It uses twice as much electricity, so I had to add two power inputs. This became an issue when I realized there were no power sockets in the forest. Nevertheless, I simply needed to invent a power bank.

Wood-Powered Power Bank

The Wood-Powered Power Bank© allows for chopping of many trees. You can power your electric chainsaw with the trees you chop down. This project was done solo, and took 2 years of on and off work.
